Meet Commander

Commander is an app for the Pebble™ smartwatch that allows you to control your computer using preset commands, from the comfort of your own wrist.

You can use it to launch your favorite games, use it for DIY projects, etc. It's your choice.

Let's get started

Setting up Commander is easy. All it involves is installing the Commander server and configuring it to your liking.
